You accept the offering from the figure. It is an object wrapped within a cloth rag. As you unfold the rag you glance at the figure and see no movement, yet you feel an uneasy feeling as they gaze back at you. Just as you finish unwrapping the object, you realize that you have made the biggest mistake of your life. In your hand you hold the ID of a Correctional Officer from the Ministry of Truth. As the figure removes their coat you see the tight and dark suit of the officer and in their hand they hold an electric baton. This is your end, you should have known better than to take contraband from a stranger. Goodbye User.
THE END
